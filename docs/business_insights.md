# Business Insights

## Major Insights

### Credit Amount

Credit Amount emerged as the most influential factor in predicting credit risk.

### Checking Account Status

Checking Account Status showed a strong relationship with creditworthiness, indicating the importance of financial stability.

### Credit History

Applicants with stronger credit histories generally exhibited lower risk.

### Loan Duration

Loan duration contributed significantly to model predictions and remains an important consideration in lending decisions.

## Practical Applications

Banks and financial institutions can use predictive models to:

* Support loan approval decisions
* Identify high-risk applicants
* Reduce default rates
* Improve portfolio quality
* Enhance customer risk segmentation

## Limitations

* Dataset contains class imbalance.
* Limited sample size (1000 records).
* Additional financial variables could improve performance.

## Future Improvements

* Apply SMOTE for class balancing.
* Perform hyperparameter tuning.
* Explore Gradient Boosting and XGBoost models.
* Deploy the model as a web application.
